The Anuradhapura city is one of the major tourist attraction zones in Sri Lanka. The purpose of the survey is to find factors that influence a tourist to use cycling as their primary transport method within the Sacred City. THE EVALUATION OF FACTORS INFLUENCING BICYCLE TRIPS IN A HERITAGE CITY (A CASE STUDY ON CYCLING TOURIST IN ANURADHAPURA ANCIENT CITY)

The Anuradhapura is a major district in North Central province. It was an ancient city complex in 346 BC. This is one of the major tourist attraction zones in Sri Lanka. The area is enriched with historical monuments ex: Sri Maha Bodhi, Ruwanweliseya Monastery, Thuparamaya, Lovamahapaya,Abhayagiri Dagaba, Jetavanarama, Mirisaveti Stupa, Lankarama and Archaeological Museum etc.

The Less attention is given to leisure cycling tourists in Anuradhapura heritage city. The factors that encourage foreign and local tourists in cycling is not quite available. The leisure cycling is a long to medium distance cycling trip for pleasure, experience and freedom suitable for physically capable travellers.

The cycling tourism is a soft mobility based transport method within the Sacred City. The purpose of the research is to to develop a model based on a primary data to understand foreign and local tourists requirements/ choices to develop cycling tourism infrastructure within Anuradhapura ancient city.
